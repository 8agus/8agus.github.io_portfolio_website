About Me

Meet me, a driven and highly motivated Business Banker turned Full Stack Developer with a unique perspective and set of skills. For over 10 years, I worked as a Business Banker, dealing with a wide range of businesses and their diverse operational aspects.

My responsibilities required me to have a deep understanding of how companies function, from their financials and target market to their inventory management and order placement.

This experience has made me a well-rounded Full Stack Developer, with the ability to design intuitive User Interfaces and databases. I am proficient in multiple programming languages such as Python, MYSQL, PostgreSQL, and Node.js, as well as front-end frameworks such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, I have completed courses in Discrete Mathematics, Databases, and Computer Architecture, further honing my technical skills.

As a Full Stack Developer, I am passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and methodologies. I am constantly seeking out new challenges and opportunities to expand my skills and deliver high-quality solutions that meet the needs of clients and end-users.

My strength as a collaborator and communicator has been honed through years of working in agile environments and delivering projects within tight deadlines. I firmly believe that effective communication and collaboration are essential for creating successful projects.
